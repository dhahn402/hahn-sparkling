Our Story

Founded by a man who knew thirst

Before Hahn Sparkling, there was a season I don't hide from. For sixteen months I was homeless and on the run — chasing what people in recovery call a "geographical cure": a one-way ticket all the way to Guadalajara, Mexico, believing a new place could fix an old ache. It couldn't. Geography never heals what only God can.

But by His grace, I made it home. I drank the water most people are warned about, and I was kept — protected, when so many are not. That is not a boast; it is a testimony. I know what it is to thirst — for water, for hope, for a way back. And I know Who answered.

So Hahn Sparkling was never only about premium water. It is about the living water that carried me home — and making sure the next person who thirsts is met with a hand, not a turned back.

— David Hahn, Founder
